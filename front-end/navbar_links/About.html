Our Mission

To provide pedestrian and cyclist data.

At Team Vision, we strive to provide the most accurate pedestrian counting system out there to solve the problem every City planner has had. Who is actually using this path? We are 5 La Trobe University students with eager ambitions to problem solve. Through our collaboration with La Trobe University and the City of Bendigo, we have created a pedestrian counting system that will give our users this data.

The means to know how many people are using the path on each day of the week, if they were walking or on a bicycle, and what direction they were walking in is all extremely important data that we want our users to have. Our service is easy to install, cheap to maintain, self-sustaining through the use of environmentally friendly solar panels and above all else, accurate.
