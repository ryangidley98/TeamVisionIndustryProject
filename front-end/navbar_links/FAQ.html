1. What is our objective?

Using sensors we want to record the frequency of pedestrians and cyclists along footpaths in Bendigo and their direction of travel. This data is then visualised on a website and available to download.

2. Where are the sensors placed?

The sensors are inconspicuously placed on the bottom of bridges, on trees and attached to the sides of light poles. All sensor placements locations are chosen to ensure they are unintrusive and hidden to avoid tampering.

3. What information is recorded?

Only whether the object recorded is a pedestrian or a cyclist and the direction they are moving in. No personal data or images of the people recorded is taken.

4. How long will the sensors record for?

The sensors can record indefinitely and send data to the database every 10 minutes where the website is updated immediately after.

5. How are the sensors powered?

The sensors are powered by batteries running off of solar panels.
